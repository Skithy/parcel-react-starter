# Card

With `MDX` we can define a story for `Card` right in the middle of our
markdown documentation.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Basic card">
    <Card title="Hello" subtitle="World" />
  </Story>
</Preview>

With `MDX` here's an editable edition

<Preview>
  <Story name="Editable">
    <Card
      title={text('title', 'Title')}
      subtitle={text('subtitle', 'Subtitle')}
    />
  </Story>
</Preview>
